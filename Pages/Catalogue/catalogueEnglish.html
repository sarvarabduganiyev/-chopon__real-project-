Chapan

Chapan is a caftan worn by men and women over clothing, usually during the cold winter months. It is popular in Central Asia, including Uzbekistan Afghanistan, Tajikistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan and other neighboring countries. They sew a chapan on wadding or camel wool with a cotton lining.
